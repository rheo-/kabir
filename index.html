Kabir Shahriar

Graduate Student · Tokyo Institute of Technology · Tokyo, Japan

Kabir Shahriar is currently a PhD student majoring in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at the Tokyo Institute of Technology in Tokyo, Japan. His research focus is on the application of organic semiconductor materials in medical imaging systems.

Research Experience

Fabrication of organic semiconductor devices

Fast, sensitive near infra-red imaging devices

Starting from 2017, Kabir has been experimenting with different fabrication processes of organic semiconductor devices. His current project is to prepare highly sensitive near infra-red photodetector on flexible PEN substrates.

April 2017 - Present

Carrier profiling in Schottky diodes

Study of Schottky diodes with linear and non-linear doping

For the final thesis of his Undergraduate studies, Kabir studied the carrier profile in Schottky barrier diodes for linear and non-linear doping.
